## Install as PWA (Recommended)

Install Permish directly from your browser — no App Store needed.

### iPhone / iPad

1. Open **Safari** and visit your Permish instance.
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow pointing up).
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen."**
4. Tap **"Add."**

### Android

1. Open **Chrome** and visit your Permish instance.
2. Tap the menu (**three dots**), then **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app."**
3. Tap **"Install."**

The app works offline, syncs when connected, and receives updates automatically.

### What You Get

- Full-screen standalone app (no browser chrome)
- Offline support with local data caching
- Signature capture with touch input
- Automatic updates — no store downloads needed
- Push-capable when supported by the browser
